Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, process, and appointment scheduling.

What services do you offer?

We offer a range of Automotive Paintless Dent Repair services, including dent removal, hail damage repair, and more.

How does it work?

Our skilled technicians use specialized tools to massage the metal back to its original shape from behind the panel, without damaging the original paint.

Can I schedule an appointment online?

Yes! You can easily schedule an appointment online through our website or by calling us directly. We offer flexible scheduling to fit your needs.

Do you provide a warranty?

Yes! We provide a Lifetime Warranty on all our paintless dent repair services. If any repaired dent returns due to workmanship issues, we'll fix it at no additional cost.

How long does it take?

Most paintless dent repairs can be completed within 1-3 hours, depending on the size and complexity of the damage. We'll provide you with an accurate time estimate during your consultation.
